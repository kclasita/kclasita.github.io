About Chomps

Chomps are a sideproject of mine as an escape from the computer. Originally a school project, they have been refined and now are available for download. My goal was to create something unexpected from paper: motion and sound. The finished products ended up a hardy paper toy. I also created a site to experiment with mimicking the movement go try them out!
